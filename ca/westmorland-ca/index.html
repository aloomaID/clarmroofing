Energy savings are another key benefit of metal roofing. Metal roofs are available in a variety of colors and styles, allowing homeowners in Westmorland California to select an option that complements the architectural style of their home. Unlike other roofing materials that require frequent repairs or replacements, metal roofs are virtually maintenance-free. They don’t rot, crack, or warp, saving homeowners time and money on upkeep.

Westmorland (formerly, Westmoreland) is a city in Imperial County, California. Westmorland is located 8.5 miles (14 km) southwest of Calipatria. The population was 2,014 at the 2020 census, down from 2,225 at the 2010 census, up from 2,131 in 2000. It is part of the El Centro Metropolitan Area. The mayor of Westmorland is Ana Beltran.
