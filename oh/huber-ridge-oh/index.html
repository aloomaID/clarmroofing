Huber Ridge, OH Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Huber Ridge is located in northeastern Franklin County at 40°05′34″N 82°54′58″W. Interstate 270, the beltway around Columbus, forms the northern and eastern edges of the CDP.
