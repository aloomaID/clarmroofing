Oxford is a city in and the county seat of Lafayette County, Mississippi, United States, 75 miles (121 km) southeast of Memphis. A college town, the University of Mississippi or 'Ole Miss' is adjacent to the city. Founded in 1837, it is named for Oxford, England.
