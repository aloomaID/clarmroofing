Centerfield, UT Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Centerfield is a city in southwestern Sanpete County, Utah, United States. The population was 1,367 at the 2010 census. Although Centerfield was a town in 2000, it has since been classified as a fifth-class city by state law. The community was so named because of its location in the center of a wide valley.
