Gaithersburg, MD Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Gaithersburg (/ˈɡeɪθərzbɜːrɡ/ i GAY-thərz-burg), officially the City of Gaithersburg, is a city in Montgomery County, Maryland, United States.
