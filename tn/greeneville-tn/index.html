1. Durability and Longevity
Metal roofs are known for their exceptional durability, often lasting 40-70 years, depending on the material. Unlike traditional shingles that may wear out quickly in extreme weather, metal roofing can withstand harsh conditions, including high winds, heavy rain, and the intense sun exposure typical of Greeneville Tennessee summers. 2. Energy Efficiency
Energy savings are another key benefit of metal roofing. The reflective surface of metal roofs helps reduce cooling costs during the hot Greeneville Tennessee summers by reflecting heat away from the home.
