Wolf Lake, MI Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Wolf Lake is an unincorporated community and census-designated place (CDP) in Muskegon County in the U.S. state of Michigan. At the 2020 census, the population of Wolf Lake was 5,034. As an unincorporated community, Wolf Lake is governed by Egelston Township. The community lies 4 miles (6.4 km) east of the city of Muskegon.
