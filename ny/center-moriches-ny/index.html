Center Moriches, NY Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Center Moriches (/moʊˈrɪtʃɪz/ i moh-RITCH-iz) is a hamlet and census-designated place in Suffolk County, New York, United States. The population was 7,580 at the 2010 census. Center Moriches is in the town of Brookhaven. It is the location of the historic Masury Estate Ballroom and Terry-Ketcham Inn, both listed on the National Register of Historic Places.
